Foundation engineering in Overland Park must address the region’s expansive clay soils and shale bedrock, which drive specific requirements under the International Building Code as adopted by Johnson County. Our category covers geotechnical investigation, bearing capacity analysis, and settlement control tailored to these conditions, with a strong focus on deep foundation solutions. For structures on highly plastic soils, [pile foundation design](piles) provides reliable load transfer to competent rock or stiff strata, mitigating shrink-swell risks that commonly affect shallow footings.

This expertise supports projects ranging from commercial mid-rises and municipal buildings to custom residential construction on challenging slopes. When soil improvement is preferable to deep foundations, we integrate ground modification strategies through [ground improvement](ground-improvement) services. For sites requiring excavation support adjacent to existing infrastructure, our [excavation support design](excavation-support-design) ensures safe construction while protecting neighboring properties in Overland Park’s developed corridors.
